AI Executive Briefing is a deployed BlueAlly web application that delivers an interactive, animated AI strategy presentation to executive audiences. It replaces static PowerPoint decks with a web-native experience, using [[framer-motion]] for slide animations and [[zustand]] for slide navigation and presenter controls.

## Technical details

- **Stack**: Next.js, React, Tailwind CSS, [[framer-motion]], [[zustand]]
- **Database**: none
- **Deployed at**: ai-executive-briefing.vercel.app (via [[vercel]])
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/red11scout/ai-executive-briefing
- **Last commit**: 2026-03-28
- **Status**: deployed

## Ecosystem role

AI Executive Briefing is a standalone presentation tool and part of the broader [[blueally-presentation-toolkit]], sitting alongside [[blueally-workshop-experience]] and [[blueally-presenting]]. Together these projects represent BlueAlly's strategic shift away from PowerPoint toward web-native, animated presentation formats for executive audiences.

Unlike the [[blueally-pipeline]] apps ([[aiworkflow]], [[ai-architecture-studio]]) which generate analytical artifacts, this tool is purely a presentation layer for communicating AI strategy.

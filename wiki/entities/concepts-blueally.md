Concepts BlueAlly is an active BlueAlly internal platform hosted at concepts.blueally.ai for organizing and managing AI concepts and their relationships. Built with Next.js and [[neon]], it serves as a structured concept library for the BlueAlly team. Last committed 2026-04-16 (see [[concepts-blueally-source]]).

## Ecosystem role

An internal knowledge management tool focused on discrete AI concept organization. Sits alongside [[knowledge-capture]], a related BlueAlly internal platform oriented toward broader structured knowledge intake and retrieval. Together these tools support the team's ability to structure domain knowledge that may feed into presentation and education platforms such as [[blueally-presenting]] and [[ai-visual-intelligence-library]].

## Stack

- Framework: Next.js + [[react]]
- Database: [[neon]] (serverless Postgres)

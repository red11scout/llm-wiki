The BlueAlly Pipeline is the end-to-end sequence of tools and platforms BlueAlly uses to take a client from initial marketing contact through a fully delivered AI strategy engagement. Each step produces structured output consumed by the next.

## Pipeline stages

1. **Top-of-funnel** — [[aiwebsiteblueally]] / [[aiwebsiteblueally-next]]: marketing and lead capture.
2. **Assessment** — [[researchapp]] (discover.movefasterwithai.com): generates the assessment JSON that seeds the pipeline.
3. **Analysis** — [[aiworkflow]]: 10-step guided analysis workflow consuming assessment JSON.
4. **Architecture generation** — [[ai-architecture-studio]]: produces architecture diagrams, agentic workflows, PRDs, and financial models.
5. **Executive readout** — [[ai-executive-readout]]: polished executive-level AI readout reports.
6. **Infrastructure sizing** — [[ai-infra-sizing]]: deterministic right-sizing of enterprise AI infrastructure.
7. **Investment intelligence** — [[atlas]]: token usage projections, Private AI BoMs, ROI analysis.
8. **Education** — [[ai-visual-intelligence-library]]: animated explainers and AI literacy support for executives.
9. **Customer journey tracking** — [[blueally-customer-portal]] (planned): authenticated multi-tenant portal tracking the [[blueally-framework-journey]].
10. **Client delivery** — [[blueally-microsites]]: password-protected landing pages aggregating all generated artifacts for each client at portal.[[gofasterwithai]].com.

## Supporting tools

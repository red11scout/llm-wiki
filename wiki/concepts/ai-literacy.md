AI Literacy refers to the foundational knowledge of AI concepts — architectures, patterns, trade-offs, and capabilities — that enables executives and decision-makers to understand, evaluate, and act on AI-generated outputs without requiring deep technical expertise.

## Role in the BlueAlly ecosystem

Within the [[blueally-pipeline]], AI literacy is explicitly addressed by the [[ai-visual-intelligence-library]], which provides animated explainers, system maps, and decision-grade comparisons designed to bring executives up to speed on the concepts underlying pipeline outputs such as use-case assessments ([[aiworkflow]]), architecture diagrams ([[ai-architecture-studio]]), and infrastructure sizing ([[ai-infra-sizing]]).

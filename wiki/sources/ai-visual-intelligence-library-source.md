## Description (verbatim)

Interactive executive education platform for modern AI concepts, featuring animated explainers, system maps, and decision-grade comparisons. Includes a Claude-grounded Ask drawer for contextual Q&A. Built with 4 MVP concept pages and a platform grammar designed to scale to 12+ pages.

## Ecosystem role

Educational arm of the [[blueally-pipeline]] ecosystem at `library.gofasterwithai.com`. Provides foundational [[ai-literacy]] content that helps executives understand the concepts behind the assessments and architectures generated by the core pipeline tools.

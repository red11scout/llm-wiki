Neon is a serverless Postgres database platform used as the primary database layer across the BlueAlly suite of applications. It is also used in [[fourcups]], a personal project by the same author, confirming broad adoption of Neon beyond the BlueAlly commercial ecosystem.

## Usage in BlueAlly

Neon appears as the database backend in numerous BlueAlly apps including [[aiworkflow]], [[ai-executive-readout]], [[blueally-portal]], [[blueally-microsites]], [[blueally-workshop-web]], [[concepts-blueally]], [[aiwebsiteblueally-next]], and others throughout the [[blueally-pipeline]].

## Usage outside BlueAlly

- [[fourcups]] — personal/religious Next.js application by the same author, deployed at fourcups.vercel.app

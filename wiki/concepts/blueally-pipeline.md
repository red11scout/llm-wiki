The BlueAlly Pipeline is the end-to-end AI consulting and delivery process BlueAlly uses to take an enterprise client from initial awareness through to deployed AI strategy artifacts and ongoing support. Each step in the pipeline is backed by a dedicated BlueAlly application.

## Pipeline stages

1. **Top-of-funnel** — [[aiwebsiteblueally]] / [[aiwebsiteblueally-next]]: marketing website entry point.
2. **Workshop facilitation** — [[blueally-workshop-web]], [[blueally-workshop-experience]], [[blueally-presenting-workshop]], [[blueally-portal]]: scheduling, content delivery, and participant access.
3. **Assessment (Step 1)** — [[researchapp]]: generates structured assessment JSON (smart-report-ai at discover.movefasterwithai.com).
4. **Forensic opportunity research** — [[compass]]: CFO-oriented deep-dive [[forensic-ai-opportunity-analysis]] adding financial validation rigor beyond the standard assessment flow.
5. **Guided analysis (Step 2)** — [[aiworkflow]]: 10-step guided analysis workflow consuming assessment JSON.
6. **Artifact generation (Step 3)** — [[ai-architecture-studio]]: produces architecture diagrams, agentic workflows, PRDs, and financial models from aiworkflow output.
7. **Executive readout** — [[ai-executive-readout]]: polished executive-level AI readout reports.
8. **Infrastructure sizing** — [[ai-infra-sizing]]: right-sizes enterprise AI infrastructure.
9. **Investment intelligence** — [[atlas]]: token usage projections, Private AI BoMs, ROI analysis.
10. **Financial modeling** — [[blueally-private-ai-investment-planner]]: models financial implications of private AI infrastructure deployments.
11. **Executive education** — [[ai-visual-intelligence-library]]: animated AI concept explainers with Claude-grounded Ask drawer.
12. **Customer portal** — [[blueally-customer-portal]]: tracks each organisation's [[blueally-framework-journey]] (planned).
13. **Client delivery** — [[blueally-microsites]]: password-protected client portal at portal.gofasterwithai.com linking clients to all generated artifacts.

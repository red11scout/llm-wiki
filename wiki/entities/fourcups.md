Four Cups is a personal/religious web application built by Drew Godwin, deployed at fourcups.vercel.app. It is not part of the [[blueally-pipeline]] or BlueAlly client ecosystem, but shares the same core technology stack — Next.js, [[neon]], [[drizzle-orm]], [[react]], [[tailwindcss]], and [[framer-motion]] — as the BlueAlly suite of tools.

## Ecosystem role

Four Cups sits outside the BlueAlly product ecosystem. Its existence confirms that the Next.js + [[neon]] + [[framer-motion]] stack is used by the same author for personal projects as well as commercial BlueAlly tooling.

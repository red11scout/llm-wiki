The Anthropic SDK is Anthropic's official library for programmatic access to Claude models. It is the AI integration layer across the BlueAlly ecosystem, personal projects, and external-client tools built by the same author. Its role ranges from core generative intelligence (consulting brief generation, architecture planning) to advisory and encouragement layers that supplement deterministic logic.

Vercel is the cloud deployment platform used to host BlueAlly's deployed web applications. Multiple tools in the [[blueally-pipeline]] and [[blueally-presentation-toolkit]] are served from Vercel-hosted URLs.

## Usage in BlueAlly

- [[ai-executive-briefing]] — deployed at ai-executive-briefing.vercel.app.
- [[ai-executive-readout]] — deployed at ai-executive-readout.vercel.app.
- Additional pipeline apps may be surfaced via the [[gofasterwithai]] branded domain.

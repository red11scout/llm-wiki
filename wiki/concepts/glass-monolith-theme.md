The Glass Monolith theme is the visual design identity used in [[blueally-microsites]]. It applies glass-morphism styling to client landing pages and pairs it with [[framer-motion]] stagger animations on page load. The theme gives client-facing portals a consistent, premium aesthetic aligned with BlueAlly's executive-audience positioning.

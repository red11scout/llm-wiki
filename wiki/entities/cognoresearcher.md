CognoResearcher is an archived BlueAlly AI Strategic Assessment Platform that explored combining cognitive analysis with strategic research capabilities in a single tool. It was not deployed to production and last saw commits on 2026-02-19.

## Stack

Built on the same Express/Vite/Drizzle stack as other early-generation BlueAlly tools: [[react]], [[vite]], [[express]], [[tailwindcss]], [[drizzle-orm]], [[neon]], [[anthropic-sdk]], and [[framer-motion]].

## Ecosystem role

CognoResearcher sits in the evolutionary lineage of the [[blueally-pipeline]] assessment layer. It followed [[cognition-two]] (the earliest multi-agent implementation) and preceded [[researchapp]], which became the canonical step-1 pipeline entry point. The capabilities CognoResearcher explored — cognitive analysis combined with strategic research — were eventually absorbed into [[researchapp]] and [[aiworkflow]]. It is also considered a predecessor to [[aiworkflow]] in the broader pipeline ancestry (see [[cognoresearcher-source]]).

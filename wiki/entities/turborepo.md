Turborepo is a high-performance monorepo build system developed by [[vercel]]. It enables large codebases to be structured as multiple co-located packages sharing tooling, dependencies, and build caching. Within BlueAlly, it is used as the monorepo foundation for [[atlas]].

## Related

- [[atlas]] — BlueAlly platform that uses Turborepo as its monorepo tooling
- [[vercel]] — Creator and maintainer of Turborepo; also hosts Atlas

The Anthropic SDK is Anthropic's official library for programmatic access to Claude language models. Within the BlueAlly ecosystem it is the standard AI integration layer, present in virtually every deployed and archived application.

## BlueAlly usage

The SDK appears across the full BlueAlly stack, from archived early explorations to current production apps:

- **Archived / early exploration**: [[chrisbot]], [[blueally-enablement-notebooks]], [[blueally-intelligence]], [[ai-catalyst]]
- **Active pipeline apps**: [[aiworkflow]], [[ai-architecture-studio]], [[ai-executive-readout]], [[ai-infra-sizing]], [[blueally-ai-app]], [[blueally-ai-solution-navigator]], [[blueally-private-ai-investment-planner]], [[atlas]]
- **Presentation & education tools**: [[ai-visual-intelligence-library]], [[blueally-presenting]], [[blueally-presenting-workshop]], [[aiwebsiteblueally-next]]

AI Visual Intelligence Library is a deployed [[blueally-pipeline]] educational platform, live at `library.gofasterwithai.com` (also `ai-visual-intelligence-library.vercel.app`). It provides foundational [[ai-literacy]] content for executives — interactive explainers, system maps, and decision-grade comparisons — helping users understand the AI concepts underpinning the assessments and architectures generated by the core pipeline tools.

## Purpose and role

The library serves as the educational arm of the BlueAlly ecosystem. Where tools like [[aiworkflow]] and [[ai-architecture-studio]] produce artifacts, the Visual Intelligence Library explains the *why* and *how* of the underlying AI concepts, bridging the gap between executive literacy and technical output.

## Features

- **Animated explainers** — concept walkthroughs with motion-driven narrative
- **System maps** — visual representations of AI architectures and patterns
- **Decision-grade comparisons** — structured comparisons to support executive decision-making
- **Claude-grounded Ask drawer** — contextual Q&A powered by [[anthropic-sdk]], allowing users to query concepts in-context
- **Scalable platform grammar** — launched with 4 MVP concept pages; designed to grow to 12+ pages

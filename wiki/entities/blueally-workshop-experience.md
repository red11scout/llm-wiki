BlueAlly Workshop Experience is a web-native workshop presentation application built by BlueAlly. It is part of the [[blueally-presentation-toolkit]] alongside [[ai-executive-briefing]], [[blueally-presenting]], and [[blueally-presenting-workshop]].

It complements [[blueally-presenting-workshop]], which takes a more content-management-oriented approach with database-backed slides, presenter mode, and Claude-powered editing.

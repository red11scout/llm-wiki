A consulting brief is a structured, actionable document that translates an executive's description of a business challenge into a framed AI opportunity, including recommended approaches and next steps. Within the BlueAlly ecosystem, consulting briefs are the primary output of [[blueally-ai-app]] and are designed to be suitable for executive review and project initiation.

## Purpose

Consulting briefs serve as the first concrete artifact in the [[blueally-pipeline]]: they lower the barrier for executives to engage with AI strategy by producing something tangible and reviewable without requiring a full workshop or assessment engagement upfront.

## Generation

In [[blueally-ai-app]], briefs are generated by Claude (accessed via [[anthropic-sdk]]) in response to a plain-language description of a business challenge supplied by the user. The output is structured and actionable, meaning it can be handed directly to stakeholders or used to initiate a deeper discovery process via [[researchapp]].

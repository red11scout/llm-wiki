`gofasterwithai.com` (and its sibling `movefasterwithai.com`) is BlueAlly's branded deployment domain. Deployed BlueAlly apps are assigned subdomains here as they graduate from Vercel preview URLs to production.

## Known subdomains

| Subdomain | App |
|---|---|
| `builder.gofasterwithai.com` | [[ai-architecture-studio]] |
| `library.gofasterwithai.com` | [[ai-visual-intelligence-library]] |
| `portal.gofasterwithai.com` | [[blueally-microsites]] |
| `presenting.gofasterwithai.com` | [[blueally-presenting]] (target; currently at blueally-presenting.vercel.app) |
| `discover.movefasterwithai.com` | [[researchapp]] |

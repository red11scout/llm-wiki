Vercel is a cloud platform for deploying and hosting web applications, particularly those built with Next.js. It is the primary deployment target for BlueAlly's suite of pipeline and presentation tools.

## BlueAlly deployments on Vercel

- [[ai-executive-briefing]] — interactive executive AI strategy presentation
- [[ai-executive-readout]] — executive-level AI readout report generator
- [[ai-infra-sizing]] — AI infrastructure sizing platform (ai-infra-sizing.vercel.app)
- [[gofasterwithai]] — BlueAlly's branded domain hosting pipeline apps including [[ai-architecture-studio]]

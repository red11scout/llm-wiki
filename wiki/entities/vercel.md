Vercel is a cloud deployment platform used as the primary hosting environment across multiple BlueAlly applications. It provides serverless deployment for Next.js-based apps and integrates tightly with the stack choices (Next.js, Neon, Drizzle ORM) common throughout the BlueAlly ecosystem.

## BlueAlly apps deployed on Vercel

- [[ai-executive-briefing]] — interactive animated executive AI strategy presentation
- [[ai-executive-readout]] — executive-level AI readout report generator
- [[ai-infra-sizing]] — enterprise AI infrastructure right-sizing platform
- [[ai-systems-presentation]] — interactive animated AI systems architecture presentation
- [[aiwebsiteblueally-next]] — Next.js rewrite of the BlueAlly AI marketing website

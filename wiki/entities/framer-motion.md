Framer Motion is a production-ready React animation library used pervasively across the BlueAlly project portfolio. It powers animated slide transitions in presentation platforms, stagger animations in client portals, and animated data visualizations in client-facing dashboards.

## Usage across BlueAlly ecosystem

- **Presentation tools**: [[ai-executive-briefing]], [[blueally-workshop-experience]], [[blueally-presenting-workshop]], [[ai-systems-presentation]] — slide and section transitions
- **Client portals**: [[blueally-microsites]] — glass-morphism stagger animations (see [[glass-monolith-theme]])
- **Data tools**: [[ai-executive-readout]], [[blueally-private-ai-investment-planner]], [[mativ-roi-dashboard]] — animated data visualizations and business impact metrics
- **Personal projects**: [[fourcups]], [[blueally-workshop-web]], [[ga-staff-scheduler]]

Compass is BlueAlly's AI Opportunity Research Platform, deployed at `compass.vercel.app`. It provides forensic AI use case discovery and validation targeted specifically at CFOs, offering enterprise-grade research capabilities for identifying, validating, and prioritizing AI opportunities with financial rigor.

## Purpose and positioning

Compass addresses [[forensic-ai-opportunity-analysis]] — a deeper, financially rigorous form of AI opportunity discovery beyond standard assessment flows. Where [[researchapp]] generates assessment JSON as step-1 pipeline input, Compass provides CFO-level validation: financial modeling, opportunity prioritization, and investment justification for AI use cases.

It complements the core [[blueally-pipeline]] (researchapp → [[aiworkflow]] → [[ai-architecture-studio]]) by adding a research depth layer oriented toward financial decision-makers rather than just technical or executive generalists. This aligns it thematically with [[atlas]] (AI investment intelligence) and [[blueally-private-ai-investment-planner]] (private AI financial modeling), though Compass focuses on opportunity discovery rather than infrastructure cost modeling.

## Ecosystem role

Compass sits alongside other CFO- and finance-oriented BlueAlly tools. Together with [[atlas]] and [[blueally-private-ai-investment-planner]], it forms a financially rigorous cluster within the [[blueally-pipeline]] — collectively supporting the [[private-ai-investment-modeling]] discipline from opportunity identification through to infrastructure cost and ROI analysis.

Assortment optimization is the retail and e-commerce practice of evaluating, selecting, and managing the set of products offered through a particular sales channel to maximize sales performance, margin, and customer satisfaction. It typically involves scoring candidate SKUs across multiple structured criteria — demand signals, margin, competitive positioning, and logistics fit — to determine which products to list, promote, or retire.

[[blueally-workbench]] applies AI-powered [[deterministic-scoring]] to this problem domain, providing analysts with a research desk for making data-driven Amazon assortment decisions at scale.

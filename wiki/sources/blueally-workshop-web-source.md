## Description

[[blueally-workshop-web]] is a web-based workshop management platform — the "Workshop Hub" — providing workshop scheduling, content management, and participant interaction capabilities. Neon provides database persistence; Framer Motion drives animations.

## Ecosystem role

Serves as the workshop management and logistics layer in the [[blueally-pipeline]]. Coordinates scheduling and administrative features, complementing the pure-presentation tools ([[blueally-workshop-experience]], [[blueally-presenting-workshop]]) with operational and participant management capabilities.

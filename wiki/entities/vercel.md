Vercel is the primary cloud deployment platform used across the BlueAlly ecosystem and related personal and client projects. It hosts the majority of deployed Next.js applications, providing serverless functions, edge middleware, and CI/CD integration.

## Known deployments

BlueAlly and related apps deployed on Vercel include: [[atlas]] (atlas.vercel.app), [[blueally-microsites]] (portal.gofasterwithai.com), [[aiwebsiteblueally-next]], [[blueally-private-ai-investment-planner]], [[ai-visual-intelligence-library]], [[ga-staff-scheduler]], [[fourcups]] (fourcups.vercel.app), [[mativ-roi-dashboard]] (mativ-roi-dashboard.vercel.app), and others across the [[blueally-pipeline]].

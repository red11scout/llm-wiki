## Description

Next.js rewrite of the BlueAlly AI marketing website, adding [[payload-cms]] for content management, [[neon]] for database persistence, and [[anthropic-sdk]] (Claude SDK) integration. Planned as the production replacement for the Replit-hosted [[aiwebsiteblueally]] (current site), incorporating the official BlueAlly brand palette and full CMS capabilities.

## Ecosystem role

Serves as the next-generation top-of-funnel marketing website for [[blueally]] AI services. Once promoted to production, it will replace the current Replit-hosted site on [[vercel]] as a Next.js application with CMS-managed content, driving prospects into the [[blueally-pipeline]] assessment pipeline.

Meharry Medical College is the client organisation for the [[meharry-testbank]] project. It is one of the historically Black medical schools (HBCU) in the United States, located in Nashville, TN, and focuses on healthcare education and medical training.

Within this wiki it appears solely as the recipient of the [[meharry-testbank]] external-client engagement — a deployed educational test bank platform for structured assessment materials and student interaction tracking.

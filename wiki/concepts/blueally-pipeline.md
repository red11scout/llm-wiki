The BlueAlly Pipeline is the end-to-end delivery sequence through which BlueAlly takes an enterprise client from initial AI interest through structured discovery, analysis, architecture, executive reporting, and infrastructure planning. Each step is a discrete deployed application that hands off structured data to the next.

## Pipeline stages

| Step | App | Role |
|---|---|---|
| 0 | [[blueally-ai-app]] | Lightweight entry point; executives frame AI use cases and generate [[consulting-brief]]s before committing to a full assessment |
| 1 | [[researchapp]] | Full AI use-case assessment; generates structured JSON |
| 2 | [[aiworkflow]] | 10-step guided analysis of assessment JSON |
| 3 | [[ai-architecture-studio]] | Generates architecture diagrams, PRDs, and financial models from aiworkflow output |
| 4 | [[ai-executive-readout]] | Polished executive-level readout reports |
| 5 | [[ai-infra-sizing]] | Enterprise AI infrastructure right-sizing |
| 6 | [[atlas]] | AI investment intelligence, token projections, and ROI analysis |
| Support | [[ai-visual-intelligence-library]] | Executive AI education and concept explainers |

## Top-of-funnel

[[aiwebsiteblueally]] (and its successor [[aiwebsiteblueally-next]]) serve as the marketing entry point. [[blueally-ai-app]] sits just inside the funnel as a low-friction way for executives to generate a [[consulting-brief]] before engaging the full pipeline.

## Presentation layer

The [[blueally-presentation-toolkit]] — comprising [[ai-executive-briefing]], [[blueally-workshop-experience]], [[blueally-presenting]], and [[ai-systems-presentation]] — supports delivery and communication at various pipeline stages.

## Predecessor

[[ai-catalyst]] was an earlier multi-agent workshop analysis platform that preceded [[aiworkflow]] as the step-2 analysis tool.

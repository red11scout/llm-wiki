Concepts BlueAlly is an active internal platform hosted at concepts.blueally.ai that provides the BlueAlly team with a structured environment for exploring, organizing, and managing AI concepts and their relationships. It is built with Next.js and backed by a [[neon]] serverless Postgres database, with [[drizzle-orm]] for type-safe data access and [[tailwindcss]] for styling.

## Ecosystem role

Concepts BlueAlly is an internal concept exploration and content management tool. It organizes AI concepts and their relationships for the BlueAlly team, and potentially feeds content downstream into [[ai-visual-intelligence-library]] — BlueAlly's interactive executive education platform — as well as other tools in the [[blueally-presentation-toolkit]].

Within the broader [[blueally-pipeline]], Concepts BlueAlly sits in a supporting infrastructure role, enriching the knowledge layer that underpins client-facing educational and presentation artifacts.
